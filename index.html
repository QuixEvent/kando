External speaker

Peter Fisk

Peter Fisk is a global thought leader, bestselling author and inspiring speaker, whose career was forged in a superconductivity lab, accelerated by managing supersonic brands, shaped in corporate development, evolved in a digital start-up, and formalized as CEO of one of the world’s largest marketing network.

Peter now leads GeniusWorks, an innovative strategy accelerator, based in London. He is also Thinkers50 Global Director and a professor of leadership, strategy and innovation at IE Business School in Madrid.

He has 30 years of business experience, working with business leaders in over 300 companies and 55 countries, from Adidas, and American Express, Bosch, BNB Paribas, Cartier and Coca Cola, McKinsey to Microsoft, P&G, Virgin to Visa. His is just about to publish his ninth book titled Business Recoded later this year.
